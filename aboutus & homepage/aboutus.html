ABOUT US

GENESIS16 MOTORSPORTS is a collegiate SAE SUPRA Team which conceptualizes, designs, manufactures and test a formula student vehicle that competes in dynamic and static event in SAE INDIA SUPRA Event. Our teams is fully professionalized in designing, modelling, manufacturing, marketing, funding. etc. Our motto is to build a fully functional formula student car which can withstand in event and gain maximum points.

Struggle

The struggle is inextricably tied to the end. It takes willingness and strength to keep moving forward until your mission and vision is achieved. which at once seemed impossible. This journey of Team Genesis 16 Motorsports was full of ups and downs, but what allowed us to continue despite these difficulties are the words of motivation of our visionary. We know that just as a puzzle cannot be completed without a missing piece, a team is nothing until every team member puts their best effort into working together and breaking the barriers that define “impossible”.

Sacrifice

Sacrifices are part of life and one must not regret while making one. We believe in sacrifice for the betterment of ourselves. Each decision made is considered a sacrifice for the greater good. Teammates who put themselves on the line and sacrifice their time and capital for the greater good of the team. There is no shortcut to success, you have to work hard and never give up.

Success

Success is “The accomplishment of aim or purpose”. Success could depend on several factors such as team coordination, time management, decision making and logical thinking ability, and so on. Success provides happiness and peace of mind when all the team members put in their 100% towards a shared goal and complete it. Everyone has different opinion about success, whether it is completing a small task of finishing a book or manufacturing and assembling a part.
